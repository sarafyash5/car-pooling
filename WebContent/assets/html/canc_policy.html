Cancellation policy

If a traveler cancels 24 hours prior the pick-up time, 10% of the total trip cost will be deducted. If a traveler cancels within 24 hours prior the pick-up time, no refund. The refunds will be made by online transfer to the customer’s bank account. No cash refund requests. The amount will be refunded approximately within 7 business days.

In case customer has opted for Security Deposit:

For Daily Rentals:
1) If booking is cancelled within 24 hours of pickup time, one-day rental will be deducted and 3 % processing fee will be charged on rest of the refund amount.
2) If booking is cancelled at least 24 hours prior to pick up time, no rental will be deducted, only 3% processing fee will be charged on the refund amount.
3) If booking is cancelled at or after pick up time, it’ll be treated as no-show and no refund will be provided.

For Hourly Rentals:
No refund will be provided in case of hourly rental booking

In case customer has opted for Loss Damage Waiver (No Security Deposit):

For Daily Rentals:
1) If booking is cancelled within 24 hours of pickup time, one-day rental plus one-day loss damage waiver charges will be deducted and 3 % processing fee will be charged on rest of the refund amount.
2) If booking is cancelled at least 24 hours prior to pick up time, no rental will be deducted, only 3% processing fee will be charged on the refund amount.
3) If booking is cancelled at or after pick up time, it’ll be treated as no-show and no refund will be provided.

For Hourly Rentals:
No refund will be provided in case of hourly rental booking
